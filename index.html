Who we are.

Immigration & Tax Services with Integrity

Since 2014, Salvatus-Guerrero & Associates, PLLC has provided trusted immigration counsel for individuals, families, and businesses. We handle visas, green cards, citizenship, deportation defense, and family petitions with care and expertise. In 2021, we expanded to offer tax services for both individuals and organizations.

PRACTICE AREA

Immigration Law

We help clients navigate the complexities of immigration law with personalized strategies and clear guidance. From visas and green cards to citizenship and business immigration, we provide attentive, detail-driven service to meet each client’s unique needs.

Tax Services

We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality tax preparation services. We offer a wide range of services, including tax preparation for individuals and businesses. We also help our clients resolve their tax issues with the IRS.
