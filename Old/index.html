WHO WE ARE

With Salvatus-Guerrero & Associates, PLLC, your case is our priority. We are committed to excellence, and have been successfully representing our clients since 2014. Our law firm specializes in immigration law and provides legal services to individuals, families, and businesses. We help clients with a range of immigration-related issues such as immigrant and nonimmigrant visa applications, green card applications, citizenship applications, deportation defense, and more. With a keen understanding on the importance of family and family reunification, the law firm helps US citizens and lawful permanent residents petition their noncitizen relatives to the United States. Depending on their particular circumstances, the law firm can help with filing for, Fiance' Visa, Petition for Alien Relative, Adjustment of Status, Consular Processing, etc.

In 2021, the firm expanded its business to include tax-related services to individuals and organizations.

PRACTICE AREA

We are dedicated to helping our clients navigate the complex and ever-changing landscape of immigration law. We provide a wide range of immigration services, including visa applications, green card petitions, and citizenship applications. We understand that every situation is unique, and we work closely with each individual to develop a personalized strategy that meets their specific needs.

We have a deep understanding of the immigration process and are committed to providing our clients with the highest level of service. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and our ability to provide clear and concise guidance throughout the entire process. Whether you are an individual seeking to obtain a visa or a business looking to hire foreign talent, we are here to help.

We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality tax preparation services. We offer a wide range of services, including tax preparation for individuals and businesses. We also help our clients resolve their tax issues with the IRS.
